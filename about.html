About Me

Hey there! I'm Nicole, and this is my photo blog. I'm currently a junior at St. Francis High School with a passion for photography, travel, and adventure!

When not with my camera, I also enjoy swimming, playing water polo, spending time with my friends and family, and taking long naps.

I started this blog as part of my IBMYP Personal Project. In choosing photography as my topic for the project, I simply wanted to gain a better awareness of the aspects of photography and the methods in which I could utilize them to yield more sophisticated and exceptional photos. From a young age, the art of photography has always fascinated me. Growing up, I loved the idea of capturing snapshots of the world exactly as it was in one instant, and my travels never failed to present me with amazing photo opportunities.

After completing some research, I took a majority of the photos for my project while on vacation or out of town during the summer of 2013 through spring of 2014. The captivating landscapes of San Francisco, Alcatraz, Argentina, Turkey, and Montauk became beautiful focuses and scenery of my pictures. Through learning more about the making of a good photo, I became more aware of the aspects of photography from other photographers and began to apply them to my own photos. I hope you enjoy them.
